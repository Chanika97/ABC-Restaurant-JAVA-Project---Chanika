ABOUT US

ABC Restaurant, located in Down South Matara, offers a unique dining experience with stunning sea views. Our mission is to provide exceptional food and service in a beautiful setting.

  • Exceptional dining with ocean views.
  • Fresh, high-quality ingredients.
  • Commitment to excellent service.
  • Perfect for special occasions.
  • Convenient takeaway options.
  • Warm and welcoming atmosphere.

Join us at ABC Restaurant for a memorable meal and enjoy the best of Sri Lankan hospitality.
